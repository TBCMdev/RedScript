What is RedScript?

RedScript is a programming language that converts high level code into minecraft commands that can be executed at any time.

It significantly speeds up the development process of projects such as minigames or maps, all in vanilla minecraft. It is packed full of features, and documented in detail for people learning the language.

To accomplish this in minecraft is no easy challenge, and comes with performance drawbacks if the language is not used correctly. That is why we recommend you read the getting started page to understand the potential drawbacks that RedScript has.

RedScript is currently in beta, and isn't recommended to employ in any large scale projects as of yet. We encourage you to try it for fun and to give feedback.

If you or someone you know is knowledgable in Minecraft commands or programming, then helping to test RedScript and give suggestions would be a great help!
